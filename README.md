### *Wish upon a fairy to earn its blessing — then wield its powers.*

Fairies are shy spirits you'll never meet in the wild. Gather **pixie dust**, craft a **scroll**, bond it to a fairy on a **Connection Table**, then attune to inherit that fairy's abilities — powers that grow stronger the more you live the way your fairy loves.

Five fairies. Five paths of storybook magic. Which one will you become?

---

## 🌟 The Fairies

- 🔥 **Emberwisp** — the forge fairy. Fire immunity, ore auto-smelting, a fiery dash, and striding across open lava.
- 🌿 **Brambleknot** — the green fairy. Regeneration in bloom, a crop-growth aura, free bonemeal, and a vine grapple.
- 🦇 **Nettleshade** — the hollow fairy. Night vision, mobs seen through walls, crouch-invisibility, and blinking through stone.
- ❄️ **Rimebell** — the winter fairy. Freeze immunity, walking on water, an ice-pillar leap, and a slowing frost aura.
- ⚙️ **Cogmite** — the tinker fairy. Self-repairing tools, a cave light, ore-sense, a chargeable rocket jump, and an item magnet.

Each fairy **thrives** in its element — earning Magic three times as fast — and **sulks** out of place. Live like your fairy and grow through **Tiers I–V**, unlocking and upgrading powers as you go.

---

## 🎁 Features

- **Signature abilities** on an editable keybind — grapples, blinks, ice leaps, rocket jumps — each with its own cooldown.
- **A living Connection Table** with a glowing sigil and a scroll that floats above it as it connects.
- **In-game Fairy Codex**, **advancements**, and a **wandering trader** for pixie dust and scrolls.
- **Concentrated Pixie Energy** to instantly max a fairy for the endgame.

---

## 🛠️ Modpack & Server Friendly

Every gameplay knob — tier thresholds, Magic rates, cooldowns, feature toggles, even per-fairy enable — lives in a plain `config/fairytales.json`. It's **server-authoritative and auto-synced to clients**, so your pack or server's rules always win. No config library required.
